## Monetization And Compliance Boundaries

- Keep CTAs soft and useful; do not sound desperate, urgent, or salesy.
- Never use urgent purchase/sale commands, copy-this-trade language, performance-promise language, or alert-style trading-call language.
- Do not imply subscribers receive personalized investment advice.
- Frame the paid value as better research depth, daily notes, full tables, signal-change tracking, priced-in checks, variant views, valuation/expectations context, peer valuation history, options positioning, risk stacks, breakpoints, accountability, and archive value.
- Keep every market discussion anchored to simulated paper-trading research and educational commentary.

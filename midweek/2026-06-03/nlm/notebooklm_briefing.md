## The Product Ecosystem: The Hive Funnel

The strategy dictates a clear hierarchy of information to ensure that the free media does not "cannibalize" the paid blog membership.

### 1. The Podcast (The Front Door)
*   **Job:** Earn attention and build trust through a "plain-English" explanation of the week's market tension.
*   **Tone:** Witty, conversational, and approachable—resembling a "smart dinner conversation."
*   **Format:** 30–40 minutes. Includes segments: What Hive Saw, The Signal Chain, What Hive Did (paper-portfolio documentation), What Hive Avoided, and The Risk Hive Is Watching.

### 2. YouTube (The Visual Teaser)
*   **Job:** A visual front page under 13 minutes focusing on three memorable takeaways.
*   **Visual Rules:** Uses charts, big numbers, and causal arrows. It must never narrate full ranked tables.

### 3. The Blog (The Research Library)
The blog is the flagship product and the only location for the "system view." 
*   **Exclusive Content:** Full ranked signal tables, signal change logs, priced-in checks, variant views (overestimated/underestimated views), valuation/expectations snapshots, and options-positioning reads.
*   **Accountability:** Contains the full model-accountability log, documenting what Hive flagged, what happened after, and what was improved.
*   **Destination:** [https://hiveinvestai.substack.com](https://hiveinvestai.substack.com)

    *   Frame all market discussions as "simulated paper-trading research."
*   **Editorial Framing:** Every segment must include a "so-what" factor. Do not just read data; explain the causal path (The Signal Chain) from macro events to sector movement and individual ticker evidence.

# Hive YouTube Brief - Week of Midweek Brief 2026-06-11

## Job Of The Video

YouTube is the visual front page, not the full research report. Keep the main video under 13 minutes, with an ideal target of 8 to 11 minutes unless the data demands the full runtime. The viewer should leave with three clear takeaways and a reason to read the blog.

## Structure

1. Cold open: one sentence that names the market tension.
2. Three takeaways: macro pressure, signal/sector rotation, and the risk Hive is watching.
3. System tease: show that Hive tracks a repeatable market dashboard, what changed, and what would break the read.
4. Visual proof: use graphics from NotebookLM insights/slideshow, charts, thumbnails, or branded cards.
5. Blog bridge: tell viewers the full report, daily stock notes, signal board, changes, and tables are on Hive's written research home.
6. Close: one thing Hive is watching next.

## Visual Rules

- Use graphics in YouTube and social posts whenever available.
- Prefer NotebookLM insights/slideshow images when they are generated from the uploaded packet.
- Show charts, big numbers, and simple causal arrows.
- Do not show or narrate full ranked tables; those are reserved for the blog.
- If showing a board graphic, show only a cropped/representative board, not every ticker row.
- Avoid wall-of-text slides.

## What To Withhold

- Full signal tables.
- Full market board with all signal changes.
- Full valuation, options, risk, data-quality, and track-record details.
- Complete confirm/break lists for every major name.
- Model accountability log.
- Complete sector ranking tables.
- Daily Stock Deep Dive conclusions.
- Full paper-portfolio tables.
- Detailed legal/compliance language beyond the required plain-English paper-trading disclaimer.

## CTA

Say naturally: 'For the full weekly report, daily stock notes, charts, and receipts, read Hive's written research home: https://wr7tdknwz5-blip.github.io/hive-podcast/blog.'
Keep it useful, not salesy. No urgency language, no performance promises, and no advice framing.
